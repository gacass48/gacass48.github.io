For my colors, I used Realtime Colors to find colors that worked well with my selected green. I then linked 
it to Figma to work on the initial model more.
https://www.realtimecolors.com/docs/randomization?colors=131101-fefcef-154313-483C32-2F4B6C&fonts=Inter-Inter

For my contact form, I used Web3Forms to allow submissions that I can receive. I can get a name, email, and 
other information if necessary. I also have my email linked on the contact page which I learned from this W3docs links.
https://www.w3docs.com/snippets/html/how-to-create-mailto-links.html
